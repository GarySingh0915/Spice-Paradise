Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 400 grams boneless chicken cut into 1½ inch pieces
  • 1 teaspoon Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons ginger paste
  • 2 teaspoons garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 teaspoons mustard oil
  • 12 medium chopped tomato
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • 1 gram mace
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried, roasted and crushed fenugreek leaves (kasoori methi)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons fresh cream

Steps

  1. Prick the chicken pieces all over with a fork. Place them in a large bowl and add the salt, red chilli powder and lemon juice and mix well.
  2. Cover the bowl with a cling film and set aside to marinate for thirty minutes in a refrigerator.
  3. For the marinade, mix together yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste, chilli powder, salt and garam masala powder. Add the mustard oil and mix.
  4. Add the chicken and mix well so that all the chicken pieces are well coated with the marinade. Cover the bowl with cling film and place it in the refrigerator to marinate for about two hours. For the gravy puree the tomatoes till smooth.
  5. Heat two tablespoons of butter in a deep non stick pan on low heat. Add the green cardamoms and mace and cook till fragrant. Add the ginger paste and garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  6. Add the pureed tomatoes, salt, red chilli powder and cook, stirring occasionally, for fifteen to twenty minutes. Preheat the gas tandoor.
  7. Open the lid and lightly grease the plate. Keep the chicken pieces, cover with the lid and cook till the chicken pieces are done.
  8. Add the roasted and crushed kasuri methi to the gravy and mix. Add the remaining butter and cook for a few minutes. Add the honey and cream and mix well.
  9. Cook for two minutes more. Add the chicken pieces and cook for three minutes. Serve hot with naan.

Chicken Momos

Ingredients

  • 1 cup minced chicken
  • 2 cups refined flour
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 chopped spring onions with greens
  • 1 inch piece chopped ginger
  • 2 chopped green chillies
  • 5-6 cloves chopped garlic
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce

Steps

  1. Combine flour and salt in a bowl, mix well. Add sufficient water and knead into a soft dough. Combine minced chicken, spring onions, ginger, green chillies, garlic, soya sauce and salt together and set aside.
  2. Divide dough into sixteen small portions. Shape them into balls and roll out thinly. Put a teaspoon of the filling in the middle. Bring all edges together to the centre, making small tight pleats.
  3. Pinch and twist the pleats to ensure that the momo is closed tightly. Steam in a steamer for about twenty minutes.
  4. Serve hot with tomato-garlic sauce.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 600 grams boneless chicken
  • 1 1/2 cups Basmati rice
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 1 inch stick cinnamon
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 5-6 cloves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 3-4 green cardamoms
  • 2 medium sliced onions
  • 3 slit green chillies
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 medium chopped tomatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • 3/4 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 cup thick yogurt
  • 3 medium sliced and fried onions
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • A few torn fresh mint leaves and coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 boiled and sliced eggs

Steps

  1. Cut chicken in one inch pieces. Boil rice and set aside. Heat oil in a non-stick pan, add cinnamon, bay leaves, cloves, cumin seeds, green cardamoms and sauté for half a minute.
  2. Add sliced onions and sauté. Add slit green chillies and chicken cubes and continue to sauté. Add turmeric powder and mix. Add tomatoes and salt and mix. Add ginger-garlic paste, red chilli powder and yogurt. Mix well.
  3. Add half the fried brown onions. Add the boiled rice on top. Sprinkle garam masala powder, ginger strips, mint leaves and coriander leaves. Sprinkle milk, rose water and remaining browned onions.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat for about ten minutes. Garnish with the egg slices and serve hot.

Chicken Kebab

Ingredients

  • 400 grams chicken leg, boneless cut into thick strips
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • Pinch of nutmeg powder
  • 1 teaspoon green cardamom powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon mace powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 3/4 cup yogurt
  • 3/4 cup fried onions
  • Salt to taste
  • Few fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  • 4-5 pieces hot charcoal
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2 teaspoons ghee
  • Oil to grill
  • Salad to serve
  • Lemon wedges to serve
  • Green chutney to serve

Steps

  1. Take chicken strips in a large bowl, add ginger-garlic paste, nutmeg powder, green cardamom powder, mace powder, garam masala and yogurt.
  2. Put fried onions in a grinder jar and grind into a coarse mixture. Add this to the chicken mixture and mix everything well.
  3. Add salt. Squeeze the juice of ½ lemon. Chop a few mint leaves and add and mix everything again till well combined. Add mustard oil and mix well.
  4. Make a well in the centre of the chicken mixture. Take burning charcoal pieces in a small stainless steel bowl and place it in the well. Keep cloves over the coal and drizzle ghee and cover to let the smoky flavour infuse for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Remove the bowl with charcoal and keep the chicken in the refrigerator to marinate for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Thread the chicken pieces onto skewers.
  7. Heat a non-stick grill pan and drizzle oil on it. Place chicken skewers, a few at a time, on it and grill on high heat for 1-2 minutes on each side. Reduce heat and cook till the chicken is 8-10 minutes or till cooked completely from all sides. Similarly cook all the chicken strips.
  8. Keep a steel bowl with burning coal pieces on a plate, drizzle some oil on it and cover it with a bamboo steamer top. Place the chicken skewers on it and serve hot with salad, lemon wedges and green chutney.

Chicken Tikka Roll

Ingredients

  • Refined flour
  • 250 grams boneless chicken
  • 1/2 inch ginger piece
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 4 chopped green chillies
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 2 medium sliced onions
  • 1 medium carrot cut into thick strips
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 4 tablespoons green chutney
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Grind together ginger, garlic and two of the green chillies into a paste. Marinate chicken cubes in ginger-garlic-green chilli paste and salt for an hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chicken cubes till done. Add cumin powder, chaat masala and check seasoning. Mix sliced onions and carrot strips.
  3. To make rolls, put one teaspoon of oil on a tawa and lightly heat the rotis. Smear each roti with one tablespoon of green chutney, place six to eight pieces of cooked chicken and sprinkle with remaining green chillies and coriander leaves. Spread a spoonful of onion and carrot mixture on the roti and sprinkle salt.
  4. Roll the roti tightly over the stuffing and serve.

Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients

  • 750 grams chicken on bone, cut into medium size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon green cardamom powder
  • Black pepper powder to taste
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven at 180ºC. Grease a baking tray with oil.
  2. Make incisions on the chicken pieces and place them in a bowl. Add lemon juice and salt and mix well. Set aside to marinate for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat ghee in a non-stick pan. Add garam masala powder, ½ teaspoon chilli powder and green cardamom powder, mix well and heat for a minute.
  4. Reserve some of the spiced ghee and add the remaining to the chicken. Mix well.
  5. Add pepper powder, ginger-garlic paste and salt and mix well. Add remaining chilli powder and oil and mix well. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  6. Place the chicken on the baking tray, put in the preheated oven and bake for 40-45 minutes.
  7. Reheat the spiced ghee and brush the tandoori chicken with it. Serve hot garnished with fresh coriander sprigs, lemon wedges and ring onions.
